What is Buyn?

Buyn is a social platform that aims to redefine the online shopping experience. One feature that we want to deliver is to make your group purchases easier by being the platform to collate your orders in a Bubble.



Wait wait...do I need to pay for this?

Not at all!



What is a Bubble?

A Bubble is a shopping cart that can be created by anyone. You will be able to share your Bubble with your friends to join you to purchase items from the same retailer.



Private vs Public Bubbles

Private Bubbles are Bubbles that will only be shared with people you want to share with.
Public Bubbles are Bubbles are opened to the public to join in on your purchase. No friends interested to join your purchase? No worries!



Why is each Bubble only constrained to one retailer?

Utilizing a multi-user single-basket approach, we will be able to share extra costs (such as shipping) together!

In order to enjoy the benefits of MUSB, the items will still be delivered to one location. However, this delivery location will be agreed by the users in the Bubble. If everyone agrees to have it delivered to your home, by all means!



How do I make a collection?

If it is a private Bubble, one person can be incharge of passing the items to everyone in the Bubble!
If it is a public Bubble, every item will have an individual tag that is attached to you. When collecting, display your tag(which will be given to you) to collect only your item!



How do i share my Bubble

Each Bubble will have an invite link that you can send to your friends!



How do i Pay?

Payment will only be made when the Bubble is filled. The final purchase will be made when everyone has paid.



What platforms will this be out on?

While we are currently working on a webapp, we will be conducting tests with other available platforms. Applications for iOS and Android are scheduled to be made in the future to make using Buyn much more easier!
